DeFi From First Principles

Detailed curriculum for protocol-side engineers who want mechanism-level DeFi understanding.

How To Use This Curriculum
  • Spend 8-12 hours per week. Each module is designed as a compact study sprint.
  • Read module README first, then the visual.html for deep explanatory content and examples.
  • Write one short mechanism memo per module: state, invariants, revenue path, failure path.
  • Prioritize Modules 02, 04, 05 for your stated end goal (Uniswap v4, perps, prediction markets).
Expected Outcomes
  • You can explain why protocols fail under stress without relying on hindsight narratives.
  • You can compare perp venues by liquidation and insurance mechanics, not surface UX.
  • You can evaluate prediction market quality by resolution design and dispute economics.
  • You can reason about Uniswap v4 hook flexibility versus added attack surface.
