Module 08 Deep Dive: Governance and Token Valuation

How control and cash flow create or destroy value

Simple Start

Imagine a business earns money. Value to shareholders depends on how much profit is distributed, reinvested, or wasted. DeFi governance is similar: tokenholders vote or delegate power to decide fee distribution, emissions, and treasury policy.

Three Layers of Value

  1. Usage value: protocol generates fees from real activity.
  2. Capture value: what portion reaches tokenholders?
  3. Governance credibility: can policy be changed unpredictably by concentrated actors?

Running Numbers

If protocol nets 120M/year and distributes 35%, then 42M reaches token stakers. With 300M circulating tokens, flow/token is 0.14 yearly before risk discount.

If distribution drops to 20%, flow/token becomes 0.08. Same usage, different token economics.

Common Beginner Mistake

Confusing "protocol volume" or "gross fees" with "tokenholder cash flow." Value accrual might be much smaller after incentives, emissions, and treasury allocation.

Governance Risk Checklist

  • How concentrated are votes and delegates?
  • Can emergency powers bypass normal process?
  • Are upgrade keys decentralized and time-locked?
