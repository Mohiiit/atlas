Module 05: Prediction Markets

Price as probability, but only if resolution is trustworthy

flowchart TD
  A[Question creation] --> B[Resolution criteria published]
  B --> C[Traders buy YES/NO claims]
  C --> D[Price discovery]
  D --> E[Event date arrives]
  E --> F[Oracle/reporting input]
  F --> G{Dispute window?}
  G -->|No dispute| H[Finalize outcome]
  G -->|Dispute| I[Escalation arbitration]
  I --> H
  H --> J[Payout 1 to winning side]
  • YES token trades at 0.63 => implied ~63% probability.
  • Buy 1,000 YES costs 630.
  • If outcome true, payout = 1,000.
  • Net profit = 370 before fees.
  • Question wording is binary and objective.
  • Oracle source is transparent and replayable.
  • Dispute process is expensive to abuse.
