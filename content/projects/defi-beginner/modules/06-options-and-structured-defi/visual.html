Module 06: Options and Structured DeFi

Yield wrappers are payoff transformations, not free money

flowchart TD
  A[Users deposit ETH] --> B[Vault writes call options]
  B --> C[Collect premium]
  C --> D[Expiry settlement]
  D --> E{Spot > strike?}
  E -->|No| F[Keep ETH + premium]
  E -->|Yes| G[Upside capped near strike]
  F --> H[Distribute yield]
  G --> H
  • Vault holds 1 ETH, sells 2,500 strike call for 80 USDC.
  • If expiry spot 2,300: vault keeps ETH and premium.
  • If expiry spot 2,900: upside past strike largely forfeited.
  • Yield source = short convexity / sold upside.
