Module 09 Deep Dive: Protocol Teardown Method

How to compare real protocols without getting lost in branding

Why Teardowns Matter

After learning individual concepts, you need a repeatable method for comparing live systems. This module gives that method so you can analyze any new protocol launch quickly.

Teardown Template

  1. State map: what are key balances, debts, positions, and risk metrics?
  2. Action map: what can users/keepers/governance do?
  3. Invariant map: what must stay true for solvency and fairness?
  4. PnL map: who pays whom in normal and stress conditions?
  5. Failure map: what breaks first under volatility and congestion?

Apply to Your End Goals

  • Uniswap v4: compare plain pools vs custom hooks. What new edge and new risk do hooks create?
  • Perps: compare matching and liquidation models in Hyperliquid, dYdX, GMX.
  • Prediction markets: compare question quality and dispute flow in Polymarket vs Augur-style models.

Deliverables You Should Write

  1. 2-page memo per protocol family
  2. State-transition diagram
  3. Failure mode table with severity and likelihood
  4. One concrete design improvement proposal
