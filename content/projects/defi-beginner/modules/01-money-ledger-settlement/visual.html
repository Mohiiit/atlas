Module 01: Money, Ledger, and Settlement

How collateralized credit and finality assumptions produce or destroy solvency

Why This Matters
  • Every DeFi protocol is ultimately a settlement system with constraints.
  • Credit-like behavior appears whenever users mint/borrow against collateral.
  • Most crises are settlement timing mismatches, not arithmetic mistakes.

Collateralization transforms credit risk into liquidation execution risk. If liquidations are fast and deep enough, protocol remains solvent. If they lag, bad debt accumulates.

Worked Examples

Example 1: 150 collateral backing 100 debt => 150% ratio. Collateral falls to 120 => 120%. If threshold is 125%, position breaches and must be liquidated.

Example 2: If oracle updates every 30 seconds and market drops 20% in 10 seconds, liquidations start late; queue pressure determines whether debt is covered.

Failure Modes
  • Oracle stale while market moves rapidly.
  • Liquidator capital insufficient under correlated crashes.
  • Gas congestion blocks risk-off actions during peak stress.
What To Monitor
  • System-wide collateral ratio distribution.
  • Oracle heartbeat and deviation triggers.
  • Liquidation completion times and slippage.
