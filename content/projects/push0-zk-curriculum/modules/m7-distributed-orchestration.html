Your familiar area: this module keeps observability light and emphasizes queue/consistency semantics and fault handling.

Core mechanics to master

  • At-least-once delivery + idempotent consumers.
  • Ack-after-publish patterns to avoid work loss.
  • Dead-letter queues + retry ceilings.
  • Backpressure across producers, workers, and queues.
  • Barrier completion across multi-input collectors.
