Kardomom
Fintech
Browser-based credit card optimizer for India. At the point of purchase, recommends which card from your portfolio gives the best reward. No real competitor exists. Built on a Python scraper (Gecko), FastAPI backend, and SolidJS frontend. Core challenge: normalizing reward points across banks and mapping merchant categories.
🔒
Cloak
Starknet
Privacy-first smart wallet on Starknet. Tongo ZK shielded payments, agent wallets, x402 micropayments, and guardian-controlled key delegation — all in one SDK (@cloak-wallet/sdk). Mobile, web, and extension apps are the reference implementation. First privacy-preserving x402 on Starknet.

MEV on Polygon / Polymarket
MEV
Front-running or back-running trades on Polymarket — a prediction market running on Polygon. Explore how market orders, liquidity, and order flow can be exploited using MEV strategies. Extends to general Polygon MEV: DEX arbitrage, sandwich attacks, liquidations. Early-stage idea, no clear architecture yet.
